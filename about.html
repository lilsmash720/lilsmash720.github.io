Who can it be knockin at my door?

Hello again websurfer. So, you want to learn about Smash, huh? Well you came to the right place! Because Smash is ME! I'M SMASH! THAT'S MEEE!

Let's start from the beginning. I was born a month before the towers fell. My family never really had computers when I was growing up. Not until about middle school. Despite that, computers and internet culture had been a big part of my life for a long time. Since I was in 1st grade. It was pretty hard for me to ignore the fact that it existed.

Flash foward to today, I am a big admirer for retro internet, early internet culture, classic 2000's culture, early 2010's video games, and just nostalgia in general.
